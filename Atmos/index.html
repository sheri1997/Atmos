About Us

Atmos aspires to provide a solution to the unaddressed problem of air-borne infection containment apart from filtering the air as an air purifier. It caters to the need of medical centers where high-tech and reliable products are essentially required and also to the common households where air pollution is a misery.

Our Solution

A portable bio-medical system capable of air purification by creating a negative or positive localised pressure. With the help of pressure difference, we facilitate a continuous supply of fresh air and create a defined area to contain and filter out the contaminated air, thus providing a safe cover to the patient from cross contamination.

"Atmos ensures all-round safety from cross contaminated air"

FILTER

Pre-Filter EU5 is used to trap dust and pollen. HEPA Filter EU13 eliminates 99.97% fine particles of size up to 0.3 micron.

ADVANCED FILTERATION

Activated Carbon filter to remove odor and toxic elements. UV treatment to deactivate 100% viruses and bacteria.

ASHRAE COMPATIBLE

Air changes per hour is ensured to be minimum of 12. Differential pressure in the range of 2.5Pa to 30Pa.

MULTIPURPOSE DUCT ARRANGMENTS

Used as Negative/Positive or recirculated air arrangement. Fittings to make the system capable of localized air treatment.

Practical Implementation

On the working principle of ATMOS we had build India's first negative pressure ambulance and Isolation room in collaboration Bafna Health care and IIT Ropar. The ambulance and room ensures that the infectious airborne germs discharged by the patient are appropriately handled during transportation or accommodation thus avoiding the cross contamination of the diseases.
